Echolift

Echolift is a mobile platform built to reinvent charitable giving by making it easy for people to donate essential goods and support verified charities. It enables users to browse trusted organizations, donate items such as clothing, food, or supplies, and schedule pickups directly from their location. With transparency, ease of use, and local impact at its core, Echolift makes generosity part of everyday life, not just special occasions.

  • Authentication
  • Item Donations
  • Scheduling
  • Google Maps
  • Firebase
  • API

Echolift Ambassadors

Echolift Ambassadors is a mobile application designed for charity organization representatives working with Echolift. It empowers ambassadors to manage donation requests, coordinate pickups, and oversee charitable operations on behalf of their organizations. The app provides real-time notifications for new donations, tools to track pickup schedules, and features to communicate with donors, making it easier for charities to efficiently manage their donation programs and serve their communities.

  • Authentication
  • Image Upload
  • Firebase
  • API
  • Pickup Scheduling
  • Real-time Database
